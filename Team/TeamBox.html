About ?

Welcome to TeamBox, the ultimate team chat app designed to bring colleagues closer together! Whether you're sharing ideas, collaborating on projects, or just having fun, TeamBox is your go-to platform. With seamless messaging, real-time communication, and the ability to post memories, TeamBox creates a vibrant, interactive space for teams to stay connected.

This app is proudly developed by Yasmine Ben Debba and Ben Khlifa Yassine, two passionate web development students in their final year of studies. Their mission is to make teamwork not just productive, but also enjoyable and engaging. With TeamBox, the boundaries between work and fun disappear, fostering a dynamic and collaborative environment for everyone.

Join us on TeamBox and experience the perfect blend of functionality, creativity, and connection!
